My Story...

Hi, I’m Prince, the student on a journey of discovery.

For me, the world of tech isn't about picking one lane and staying in it. It's a massive, interconnected playground of problems to solve and tools to build with. My passion lies in exploring as much of that playground as I can.

My journey so far has taken me through:

  • Web Development: Crafting simple websites with HTML and CSS.
  • Robotics: learning the basics of circuits and Arduino

I don't see these as separate fields; I see them as different languages telling the same story of innovation. My broad exploration allows me to approach problems with a unique perspective, connecting ideas from different domains to find creative and robust solutions.

I'm currently looking for opportunities to collaborate with a team that values continuous learning and isn't afraid to tackle challenges that sit at the intersection of different technologies.

If you have an interesting problem to solve, I'd love to hear about it.
